Nuts & Bolts

We successfully completed the Nuts & Bolts game with a team of two. Mathematical structures such as Bezier Curve Formulas were utilized. Additionally, we developed a tool that allows us to efficiently produce bolts in the desired shape within a short timeframe. There are approximately 20 handcrafted levels.
