Strattle

Strattle is a Multiplayer Party Game that was an ambitious project where we attempted to merge two different game concepts. However, due to the small size of our team, we were unable to complete it. While the game may not be visually polished, it includes a wealth of advanced and technically complex features. The project incorporates a Client, Server, Database, and AI server—all of which were entirely developed and integrated from scratch by our team. We built our server using Riptide Networking, and the project comprises approximately 28,044 lines of code.
